Click anything on screen — without the mouse.

Three keystrokes, no reach for the trackpad

keyouse scans the front app, the menu bar, and the Dock through the Accessibility API — the same tree VoiceOver reads — so every actionable element gets a hint.

01 · SUMMON

⌘⇧Space

A glass search panel opens and numbered badges appear over every button, link, and field on screen.

02 · AIM

Type or number

Type part of a label to filter down, or just press the badge number. Selection follows as you type.

03 · ACT

Click · right-click · scroll

Enter clicks, ⇧ right-clicks, ⇧↑↓ scrolls. The panel gets out of the way while you work.

The whole keymap

Everything is reachable from the home row. Hold a modifier to filter the hints down to just what you want.

numLeft-click the hinted element
⇧numRight-click it
⏎Click the selected element
↑↓Move the selection
⇧↑↓Scroll the area under the panel
⌘Buttons only (while held)
⌃Form fields only (while held)
⌘LLinks only (toggle)
⌃IFocus the first input field
⌘TabWindow picker (release ⌘ to choose)
⌘RRescan hints
⌘,Settings · esc cancels
